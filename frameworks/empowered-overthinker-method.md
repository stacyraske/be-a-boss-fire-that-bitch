# Empowered Overthinker Method™

**Source:** Chapter 9 (Take Your Power Back)  
**Type:** Pattern Interrupt / 4-Step Cycle

## Purpose
A rapid-response cycle for breaking sabotage loops, identifying triggers, and returning to aligned action without self-judgment.

## The Cycle
1. **AWARENESS**  
   *Pay attention.* Where am I stuck? How am I feeling? Who am I being? Catch the sabotage or trigger before it compounds.
2. **OWNERSHIP**  
   *Take responsibility without judgment.* What have I been focusing on? What am I owning that isn’t mine? What can I let go of? Reclaim power from external conditions.
3. **ALIGNMENT**  
   *Connect to truth.* Step out of "shoulds" and into presence/gratitude. Ask: *"What do I need?"* Let the first answer guide the next step.
4. **ACTION**  
   *Execute inspired movement.* Take the aligned step, even if it’s rest, delegation, or pivot. Trust the direction over the original plan.

## Canonical Principle
> *"You can’t react and act at the same time... The key is getting past the stuck point of expectations or only seeing what’s wrong."* (Ch 9)
