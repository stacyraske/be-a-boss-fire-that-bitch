# Scarcity to Abundance Shift™

**Source:** Chapters 1, 7, 10  
**Type:** Mindset Architecture

## The Core Belief Shift
- **Scarcity Baseline**: `"I am not enough."` → External validation required → Success = measurable results → Hustle = worth
- **Abundance Baseline**: `"You are enough."` → Internal validation sufficient → Success = who you’re being in the process → Ease = alignment

## Symptoms of Scarcity
- Freebie chasing, preparation purgatory, emotional constipation
- Fear of success, upper limit sabotage, conditional joy
- Measuring worth by numbers, outcomes, or external approval

## Canonical Principle
> *"When you focus on the process (how I’m showing up and who I’m being), I allow success to flow with ease and fun!"* (Ch 10)
