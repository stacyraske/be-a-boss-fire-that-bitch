# Aligned Abundance Process™

**Source:** Chapter 3 (Foundation for Success)  
**Type:** Core Methodology / 5-Step Process

## Purpose
A foundational sequence for building internal connection, releasing emotional burden, and shifting from conditional scarcity to unconditional self-trust.

## The 5 Rs
1. **Anchor Your Routines** (Ch 4)  
   Consistent daily actions that ground you, build connection to self, and replace reactive busyness with intentional follow-through.
2. **Release Your Baggage** (Ch 5)  
   Constructive emotional release techniques (journaling, tapping, write & burn, physical release) that sever triggers and dissolve emotional constipation.
3. **Respect Your Boundaries** (Ch 6)  
   Clear delineation of responsibility. The power of the internal "no." Replacing walls with permeable, protective limits.
4. **Be Open to Receive** (Ch 7)  
   Trading expectation for appreciation. Dismantling walls that block abundance, permission, and joy.
5. **Raise Your Standards** (Ch 8)  
   Elevating expectations of self, tribe, and environment. Shifting focus from "what is" to "who you are becoming."

## Canonical Principle
> *"Success really is built on a solid foundation... You’re going into your foundation, addressing the root issues compromising the integrity, restructuring and building a new foundation."* (Ch 3)
