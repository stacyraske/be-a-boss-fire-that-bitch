# Inner Critic Ecosystem™

**Source:** Chapters 1–2  
**Type:** Psychological Architecture / Diagnostic Map

## Components
- **The Inner Critic**: The voice of "not enough." Fuels scarcity, comparison, and conditional worth. Functions as a messenger of misalignment.
- **The Ego**: Constructed identity built to avoid rejection. Filters perception to confirm limiting beliefs. Separates to protect.
- **The Inner Rebel**: The authentic self pushing against curated facades. Shows up as resistance, impulsivity, or deliberate inaction when boundaries are crossed or truth is suppressed.
- **The Authentic Self**: The unconditioned baseline. Knows "you are enough." Operates in ease, flow, and alignment. Blocked by walls, released by boundaries.

## Canonical Principle
> *"Your inner critic isn’t a bitch keeping you trapped, it’s an agent notifying you of your disconnect."* (Ch 10)
